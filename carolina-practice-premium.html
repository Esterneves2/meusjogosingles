One Week Challenge — Simple Past & Present Continuous

Welcome, Carolina! This week you'll practice Simple Past (since you're working on it) and touch on Present Continuous to understand the difference. Everything is practical and in real contexts.

Each day takes only 10 minutes. You'll have prompts, examples, and space to write your own sentences. Record the final challenge at the end of the week and send it to your teacher. Let's go!

🎯 Your Final Challenge

Record yourself telling a story about your last weekend. Tell what you did (past), what you were doing (continuous), and how it was. 30-40 seconds maximum.

Example structure:
"Last Saturday, I woke up at 9 AM. I had coffee. Then I was watching TV when my friend called. We went to the park..."

Send to: Your teacher via WhatsApp
Success criteria: You used past tense, didn't freeze, tried your best!

💡 Quick Tip: Don't worry about being perfect. This is practice! Your teacher is looking for effort, not perfection. If you freeze or make mistakes, that's totally normal — just keep talking.
